Welcome to Parents’ Voice USA!

Parents’ Voice USA is a volunteer-led group founded in 2009 by MJ Noor. We empower parents by providing clear, expert-backed guidance on educational challenges—from elementary school through college.

Contact us anytime at info@theparentsvoiceusa.com to learn about upcoming events and resources.

Meet Our Team

MJ Noor

Founder

Harvard Law School alumna; Family Business Consultant; Founder of Honors Parent Association and Parents’ Voice USA. Most importantly, mother to two successful boys who graduated from Fullerton Union High School and went on to attend Berkeley and UCLA.

Andrew Wagner

Honors Math Student & Learning Assistant

Honors Math student at UCI, club founder, Learning Assistant, and aspiring lawyer. Grew up in Fullerton and witnessed firsthand how Fullerton Union High School decisions impact families—fueling our mission to be the school-related voice for parents in the district.

Abby Nordell

Political Science student at Biola University; passionate about politics and ethics. Abby has been integral to Parents’ Voice USA—vigilantly tracking educational policy and school-board decisions in the Fullerton Union High School District to give parents a clear, informed voice.
